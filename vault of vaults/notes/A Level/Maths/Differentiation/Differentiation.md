Differentiation is an algebraic process to find out the change of one thing with respect to another, or in graphical terms, the gradient of a graph at a point. This is equal to the gradient of the tangent to the graph at that point.

Taking the derivative of some value $y$* *with respect to $x$ is written as 

$$
\frac{dy}{dx}
$$

This is now the gradient of the graph $y = f(x)$. It can also be written as

$$
f'(x)
$$

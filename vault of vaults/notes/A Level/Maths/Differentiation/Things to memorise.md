\frac{d}{dx}\hspace{1mm}e^{f(x)} = f'(x)\hspace{1mm}e^{f(x)}

$$
\frac{d}{dx}\hspace{1mm}ln(f(x)) = \frac{f'(x)}{f(x)}

$$

For hypothesis testing the formatting in the answer is very important. The standard structure should be:

- Define $p$ as the proportion of the population (binomial) in the sample or define $\mu$ as the mean of the population (normal)
- State the null hypothesis $H_0$ and alternative hypothesis $H_1$
- State the distribution under $H_0$ as well as the sample size $n$ and significance level $\alpha$.
- Calculate the critical region
- Compare the values and state whether or not to reject $H_0$
- Put the conclusion into the context of the question

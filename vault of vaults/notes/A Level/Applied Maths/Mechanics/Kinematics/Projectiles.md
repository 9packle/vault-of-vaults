The general equation for the path of a projectile is
$$
y = xtan\theta - \frac{gx^2}{2u^2cos^2\theta}
$$
This comes from the displacement equations in the $x$ and $y$ directions:
$$
x = utcos\theta
$$
$$
y = utsin\theta-\frac{1}{2}gt^2
$$
The time of flight of a projectile is
$$
T = \frac{2usin\theta}{g}
$$
The maximum height of a projectile is
$$
H = \frac{u^2sin^2\theta}{2g}
$$
The maximum range of a projectile is
$$
R = \frac{u^2sin2\theta}{2g}
$$

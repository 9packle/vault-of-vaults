Kinematics is a branch of mechanics that studies the motion of a body or a system of bodies without consideration given to its mass or the forces acting on it. 

For constant acceleration questions, there are 5 suvat equations:

$s = ut + \frac{1}{2}at^2$

$s = vt - \frac{1}{2}at^2$

$s = \frac{1}{2}(u + v)t$

$v^2 = u^2 + 2as$

$v = u + at$

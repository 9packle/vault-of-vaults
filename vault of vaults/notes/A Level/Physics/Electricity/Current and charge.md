Charge is a fundamental property of particles. The fundamental charge $e = 1.60^{-19}\, \textup{C}$.The SI unit of charge is the coulomb. Any charged particle can be a charge carrier in a circuit (as long as it is free to move)

Current is the rate of flow of charge, or written as an equation:
$$
I = \frac{dQ}{dt}
$$
Current has the same value at all points in a series circuit.

The centre of mass of an object is the point where all of its weight can be considered to act.


The equation for centre of mass in a 2D plane is 

$$
(x,y)_{CM}=(\displaystyle\sum_{i=1} ^{n} \frac{m_i x_i}{m_i}, \displaystyle\sum_{i=1} ^{n} \frac{m_i y_i}{m_i})
$$
